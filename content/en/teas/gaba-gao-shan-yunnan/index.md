Gaba Gao Shan Yunnan is a unique high-mountain oolong that bridges the gap between Taiwanese craftsmanship and Yunnan's ancient tea tree heritage. Produced in the Wuliangshan Highlands using leaves from 70-year-old trees, this tea offers a powerful, relaxing experience with a rich, complex flavor profile.

## Origin

Wuliangshan Highlands, Pu'er County, Yunnan Province, China. Harvested in Autumn 2024 from large tea trees growing at an altitude of 2000 meters. The tea is processed using traditional Taiwanese GABA oolong technology and special equipment, adapted to the robust material of Yunnan's ancient trees.

## Flavor & Aroma Profile

- **Aroma**: Warm and comforting with notes of fresh bread, floral bouquets, and honey.
- **Taste**: Sweet, juicy, and soft. Reveals layers of berries, fragrant peaches, and apricots, balanced by a slight, pleasant bitterness that adds depth.
- **Finish**: Voluminous, long, and rich.

## Effects & Benefits

**Tea Superpowers:**
- Powerful intoxicating and relaxing effect
- Clarifies the mind and turns the soul specifically towards creative flight
- High GABA content offers calming benefits
- Combines the "cha qi" of Yunnan tea with the functional benefits of GABA

## Brewing Instructions

**Simple brewing method:**
- **For 300-500ml teapot**: 4-6g of tea
- **Water temperature**: 96-98°C
- **Steeping time**: 3-5 minutes

This tea is resilient and can withstand high temperatures. You can re-steep it 2-3 times.
