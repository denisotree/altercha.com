Hekai 2020 is a high-quality sheng puerh harvested from big trees in the famous Hekai mountain region. Hekai is often referred to as a "hidden gem" of the Bulang mountains, known for its ancient tea gardens and distinct orchid fragrance.

## Origin

Hekai Mountain, Yunnan, China. The region is home to some of the oldest continuous tea gardens in the world, cultivated by the Lahu people. Hekai teas are celebrated for their thick mouthfeel, golden liquor, and a unique balance of bitterness and sweetness.

## Flavor & Aroma Profile

- **Aroma**: Sweet with notes of dried fruits and a hint of wild orchids.
- **Taste**: Rich and smooth. The profile is sweet and fruity, grounded by a pleasant caramel bitterness that adds structure and complexity.
- **Finish**: Long-lasting sweetness.

## Effects & Benefits

**Tea Superpowers:**
- Concentrates the mind and improves focus
- Refreshing and uplifting
- Thick mouthfeel characteristic of Hekai old trees
- Balanced energy that is stimulating yet grounded

## Brewing Instructions

**Simple brewing method:**
- **For 300-500ml teapot**: 5-7g of tea
- **Water temperature**: Boiling water (98-100°C)
- **Steeping time**: 3-5 minutes

Perfect for a morning cup to help you focus.
